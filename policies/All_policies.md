## Policy 1: Working Hours

**Standard office hours:** Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
**Weekends:** Saturday and Sunday off.

**Flexibility expected for:**
- Tight deadlines
- Client dependencies
- Critical deployments

**Project-specific timings:** For client-facing or global projects, working hours may vary based on client time zones or delivery requirements. Manager and HR will communicate any shift or schedule changes in advance.

**Shift adjustments:** Qubiqon reserves the right to modify working hours or shift patterns based on business needs. Changes will be officially communicated.

**Questions:** Contact your Manager or HR.

## Policy 2: Leave Policy

Defines all types of leave employees are entitled to.

### Leave Entitlement Summary

| Leave Type | Entitlement (Days/Year) | Pay Status | Carry Forward |
|---|---|---|---|
| Casual Leave (CL) | 6 | Paid | No |
| Sick Leave (SL) | 6 | Paid | No |
| Earned Leave (EL) | 18 (1.5/month) | Paid | Yes (max 15) |
| Compensatory Off | 3 | Paid | No |
| Leave Without Pay (LOP) | Up to 30 | Unpaid | N/A |
| Optional Holidays | Up to 3 | Paid | No |
| Paternity Leave | 3 per childbirth | Paid | N/A |
| Maternity Leave | Up to 26 weeks | As per statutory regulations | N/A |

The leave year is the **calendar year (January–December)**.

### Casual Leave (CL) — Details

- **Entitlement:** 6 days/year
- **Purpose:** Short-term personal commitments or unforeseen situations
- **Notice required:** Apply at least 3 days in advance
- **Emergencies:** Inform Manager via email the same day
- **Without prior notice (non-emergency):** Treated as Loss of Pay (LOP)
- **Carry forward:** Not allowed; cannot be used during notice period
- **Approval:** Subject to managerial discretion and business requirements

### Sick Leave (SL) — Details

- **Entitlement:** 6 days/year
- **Purpose:** Illness, injury, or health recovery
- **Notice:** Apply in advance unless emergency; notify Manager same day for sudden illness
- **Medical certificate:** Mandatory if sick leave exceeds 3 days
- **Carry forward:** Not allowed

### Earned Leave (EL) — Details

- **Entitlement:** 18 days/year (accrues at 1.5 days per month)
- **Purpose:** Vacations, extended time off, planned events
- **Carry forward:** Up to 15 days. Anything above 15 lapses.
- **Notice:** Advance application required (except emergencies)
- **Approval:** Based on managerial discretion and workload

### Compensatory Off (Comp-Off) — Details

- **Eligibility:** Employees who work more than 4 hours on weekends or public holidays
- **Entitlement:** 3 days/year (Paid)
- **How to claim:** Submit request in sumHR with managerial approval
- **Validity:** Must be used within the same calendar year; cannot be carried forward
- **Emergency leaves:** Notify Manager same day

### Leave Without Pay (LOP) — Details

- **Eligibility:** Only after all other paid leave types are exhausted
- **Maximum:** 30 days per calendar year
- **Notice:** Must be applied in advance
- **Approval:** Subject to business needs and managerial discretion

### Optional Holidays — Details

- **Entitlement:** Up to 3 days/year (Paid)
- **Purpose:** Cultural, religious, or personal occasions
- **Notice:** Must be requested in advance and approved by Manager
- **Carry forward:** Not allowed

### Paternity Leave — Details

- **Eligibility:** Male employees who become new fathers
- **Entitlement:** 3 days per childbirth (Paid)
- **Timing:** Must be taken within 30 days of childbirth
- **Approval:** Apply in advance; Manager approval required

### Maternity Leave — Details

- **Eligibility:** Female employees with at least **80 days of service**
- **Entitlement:** Up to **26 weeks** per childbirth (including weekends and holidays)
- **Pay status:** As per statutory regulations
- **Applicable for:** First two children only
- **Approval:** Apply in advance; HR approval required
- **Miscarriage / Medical Termination of Pregnancy:** 6 weeks of Maternity Termination Leave with valid medical documentation

### How to Apply

Use the **sumHR** portal for all leave applications. Follow up with Manager and HR for approvals.
**Contact:** HR@qubiqon.io

## Policy 3: Leave Process

Step-by-step process for applying for leave.

### Step 1: Communicate Early

- Inform your Manager about planned leave
- Email Manager and CC your team
- If working with a client, inform them in advance

### Step 2: Apply in sumHR

- Open the sumHR portal, choose the correct leave type, submit request
- Manager receives and approves the request
- **Leave is only valid once approved**

### Step 3: Plan Your Absence

- Arrange a backup or handover plan
- Notify the client team if required
- Set up Out of Office replies in both Qubiqon and client email accounts
- Update any active calendars or workflows

**Note:** HR monitors all leave activities.
**Contact:** HR@qubiqon.io

## Policy 5: Work From Home (WFH)

**Default model:** Qubiqon follows a **Work From Office (WFO)** model. WFH is **not encouraged** as a regular practice.

WFH is only allowed in urgent or exceptional situations.

### WFH Approval Process

1. Discuss your situation with your Reporting Manager
2. Obtain prior approval from your Manager before working remotely
3. Keep HR (HR@qubiqon.io) in copy on the approval email for tracking and compliance

### Conditions

- WFH considered only for genuine urgent or exceptional circumstances
- Approval subject to project requirements, client commitments, and business priorities
- WFH employees must remain fully available during working hours and maintain the same professionalism, productivity, and communication as in-office

**Contact:** HR@qubiqon.io

## Policy 8: Probation

### Key Terms

- **Duration:** 3 months from date of joining
- **Applies to:** All new employees
- **Extension:** Management reserves the right to extend probation based on performance and role alignment
- **End-of-probation HR meeting:** A one-on-one meeting with HR is scheduled toward the end of probation to assess experience, clarify expectations, and provide feedback
- **Confirmation:** No formal confirmation letter is issued. **Unless specifically informed otherwise, probation is considered successfully completed at the end of 3 months.**
- **Communication about extension:** If no communication is received about an extension, the employee can consider their probation complete after 3 months

### Employee Expectations During Probation

- Use this time to learn, grow, and make an impact
- Communicate openly with Manager and HR for support
- Look out for the HR meeting invite near the end of probation

**Contact:** HR@qubiqon.io

## Policy 9: Notice Period & Termination

Defines notice periods and grounds for termination.

### During Probation (or Extended Probation)

Either party may end employment with:
- **30 days' written notice**, OR
- Payment in lieu of notice (30 days' salary based on fixed annual compensation)

The release date is approved by Qubiqon based on project and team needs.

### Post-Confirmation (After Probation)

Either party may terminate with:
- **60 days' written notice**, OR
- Salary in lieu of notice (based on annual fixed compensation)

- If the employee resigns, the **final release date is decided by Qubiqon**
- Full cooperation expected during notice period for proper handover
- **Failing to complete a proper handover may result in final dues being withheld or adjusted**

### Immediate Termination (Without Notice or Compensation)

Qubiqon may terminate without notice or compensation, after preliminary internal inquiry, if the employee is involved in:

- Serious criminal acts or offenses involving moral turpitude
- Sexual harassment (per law and company policy)
- Acts that harm the company's reputation
- Any misconduct or breach of company policies or employment terms

Such actions may result in suspension or immediate termination.

### Voluntary Disclosure Requirement

- Any history related to the above offenses **must be voluntarily disclosed** during onboarding or employment
- Failure to disclose may result in **immediate dismissal**

**Contact:** HR@qubiqon.io

## Policy 11: Cybersecurity

Qubiqon's approach to securing devices and email.

### Device Security: Microsoft Defender for Business

Installed on all company devices. Functions:
- Stops viruses, malware, and ransomware
- Detects and responds to threats automatically
- Manages security risks across all devices with real-time alerts and fixes
- Runs in the background

### Email Security: Microsoft Defender for Office 365

Protects Qubiqon emails. Functions:
- Filters phishing and spam before emails reach the inbox
- Blocks fake emails and scams with dangerous links
- Investigates and remediates suspicious email activity automatically

### Employee Onboarding Steps

1. Download the Device Onboarding Package (link in the company SharePoint)
2. Extract the package
3. Right-click on `WindowsDefenderATPLocalOnboardingScript.cmd`
4. Choose **Run as administrator**
5. Follow the instructions
6. Device is onboarded to Microsoft Defender for Business

### After Onboarding

Enable periodic scanning:
1. Open Windows Security
2. Enable periodic scan

### Final Step

Fill out the mandatory survey provided after onboarding.

**Contact:** IT Support Team — raise a Helpdesk ticket in sumHR.

## Policy 15: POSH Policy

Prevention of Sexual Harassment policy, aligned with **The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013**.

### Who Is Protected

All Qubiqon employees regardless of gender, position, or employment type:
- Full-time
- Part-time
- Contract
- Interns
- Vendors

### Definition of "Workplace"

- Qubiqon office premises
- Client locations
- Work-related travel
- Offsite meetings, events, social gatherings
- Any place connected to employment

### What Counts as Sexual Harassment

Can be physical, verbal, written, or non-verbal. Includes:
- Unwelcome physical contact, advances, or touching
- Demands or requests for sexual favors
- Sexually colored remarks, jokes, or gestures
- Sharing or showing explicit content
- Inappropriate comments about personal appearance or behavior
- Creating a hostile, humiliating, or offensive work environment

> **Key principle:** Intent does not matter — impact does. If behavior makes another person uncomfortable, it can be considered harassment.

### Who Can File a Complaint

Anyone associated with Qubiqon, including:
- Employees (permanent or temporary)
- Interns
- Vendors
- Contractors
- Clients

You do not have to be the target — you can report incidents you have witnessed.

### How to File a Complaint

1. **Send a written complaint to posh@qubiqon.io**
2. **Deadline:** Within **3 months** of the incident
3. **Include:** Names, dates, locations, witnesses, and any supporting evidence
4. **Confidentiality:** Identity and information remain strictly confidential

### Expected Conduct from Everyone

- Be respectful to everyone
- Speak up if something feels wrong
- Support teammates
- Maintain confidentiality in any case proceedings

**Full policy document:** Qubiqon POSH Policy.pdf (refer to internal SharePoint)
**Contact:** posh@qubiqon.io
